HELESA SOULARI PORTFOLIO

EMPOWERING INFORMED DECISIONS THROUGH DATA CLARITY AND INSIGHTS

Hello! I'm HELESA

I am a data analyst passionate about working with organizations that aim to make effective use of their data. I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration with a minor in Information Systems, followed by an MBA in Information Science, graduating with top honors in 2024 from the University of Toledo. From the initial ideas on what to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, analysis, and management, to the impactful presentation of insights, I can support each step of the process to enable your organization to make evidence-driven decisions.

Personal Background

I grew up in a family of engineers, where problem-solving was a natural talent applied to daily life matters. As an outstanding and dedicated student, I balanced my academic pursuits with a deep dedication to equestrian sports. Over eight years, I won numerous horse jumping medals and took on the responsibility of training and caring for my horses. This experience honed my sense of responsibility and commitment. I am now eager to leverage my skills and knowledge to drive further improvements and innovations.
